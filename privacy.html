Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 19, 2026

Your privacy is critically important to us. At Light Maintenance, we have a few fundamental principles:

  • We don’t ask you for personal information.
  • We don’t share your personal information with anyone.
  • We don’t store personal information on our servers.

1. The Plugin Itself

Light Maintenance (the WordPress plugin) runs entirely on your own server.

  • No Tracking: The plugin does not send any data back to us. We do not track how many visitors you have, what your domain is, or when you activate maintenance mode.
  • No Cookies: The plugin does not set cookies for your site visitors. It may use standard WordPress sessions to allow logged-in administrators to bypass the maintenance screen, but this is native WordPress functionality.
  • No External Calls: The maintenance page generated by the plugin does not load external fonts, scripts, or assets from our servers. Everything is self-contained within your site for maximum privacy and speed.

2. This Website & Third-Party Services

When you visit lightmaintenance.site we prioritize performance and security. To achieve this, we use industry-standard third-party infrastructure that may process limited data:

Hosting & Infrastructure (Vercel & GitHub):

Our website is hosted on Vercel and GitHub. These platforms collect standard server logs, including IP addresses, to monitor network security and ensure high availability.

Security & Optimization (Cloudflare):

We use Cloudflare to protect against DDoS attacks and improve site delivery speed. Cloudflare processes web traffic to filter out malicious bots and may use cookies to provide these security features.

Analytics (Google Analytics):

We use a performance-optimized version of Google Analytics to see how many people visit our site. We have configured this to load with a delay to protect your PageSpeed. It uses anonymous cookies to track aggregate data; we do not collect or store any personally identifiable information (PII).

3. Donations & Payments

We accept donations to support the development of this open-source project.

All donations are processed securely through Fourthwall. We do not store or process your credit card information or banking details on our servers. When you donate, you are subject to Fourthwall's privacy policy and terms of service.

4. WordPress.org Repository

If you download the plugin from the WordPress.org repository, WordPress.org may collect statistics on download counts and active installs. This data is aggregate and does not identify individual users to us.

5. Changes to This Policy

Although most changes are likely to be minor, we may change its Privacy Policy from time to time. We encourage visitors to frequently check this page for any changes. Your continued use of this site after any change in this Privacy Policy will constitute your acceptance of such change.

6. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact the developer, Sakib MD Nazmush, through the support channels provided on the WordPress.org plugin page.
