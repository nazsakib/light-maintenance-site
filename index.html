SEO Protection Standard.

Putting a site under maintenance can ruin your Google rankings if done wrong. Returning a "200 OK" status while your site is empty tells Google your site is broken.

Light Maintenance automatically serves a 503 Service Unavailable status. This signals search engines that the downtime is temporary, preserving your hard-earned SEO ranking.

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about the plugin.

Is this plugin really free?

Yes, 100% free and open source. If you find it useful, you can support the developer here.

Will this affect my SEO?

No. In fact, it protects it. We send a 503 Service Unavailable HTTP header, which tells search bots to come back later rather than de-indexing your empty site.

Does it work with Elementor/Divi/etc?

Yes! Since Light Maintenance operates at the server initiation level, it blocks access before your page builder even loads, saving server resources.

Can I still work on my site while it's enabled?

Yes. Logged-in administrators bypass the maintenance screen automatically. You can edit your site, test changes, and view pages normally while visitors see the maintenance page.

Is it compatible with caching plugins?

Absolutely. Light Maintenance works seamlessly with WP Rocket, W3 Total Cache, Autoptimize, and server-side caching. It ensures users don't see a cached version of your site until you are ready.

How do I customize the maintenance page?

You can easily change the headline, description text, and background colors directly from the plugin settings. No coding is required to match your brand's look.
